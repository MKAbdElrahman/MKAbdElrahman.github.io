Modular Monolith

Layered Architecture Link to heading

  • Organize software around technical concerns (ui, database, business).

  • good when the technical concerns are the points of variation with a steady business logic.

  • ideal for teams organized around technical concersn (ui team, …)

  • bad for adding new features, all the layers needs to change and all technical team have to cooperate.

Modular Monolith Link to heading

  • Organize software around coherent usecases or domain concerns

  • good when the domain concerns are the points of variation.
